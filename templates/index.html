About the Project

The Text to Hand Sign Converter is an innovative tool designed to empower communication between the hearing and deaf communities. By translating text into hand signs, we make interactions more inclusive and accessible.

How It Works

Input your text, click convert, and watch as your words transform into hand sign language in real-time. This user-friendly tool is perfect for learning or for quick, on-the-go communication.
